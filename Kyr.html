How to File a Complaint

You have two main options to report a rights violation:

1
File an Online Complaint

Visit the official national or state human rights portal to submit your complaint digitally. You can upload supporting documents, track the status of your case, and receive updates via email or SMS — all without leaving your home.

or
2
Visit Your Nearest Police Station

For urgent matters or if you prefer in-person assistance, visit your local police station to file an FIR (First Information Report). You may also approach a magistrate's court directly if the police are unresponsive.
